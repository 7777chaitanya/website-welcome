Questions related to Real Dev Squad

Question - What is Real Dev Squad?

Answer - Real Dev Squad is a community of developers, where we come together to learn, use that in building projects, prepare for interviews, help each-other, put those projects online and try to help the set of new developers or freshers, helping them get better jobs and higher salaries with quality work. We come from all walks of lives and all kinds of background, with one thing in common: Wanting to work with others and improve ourselves.
